Soobeen Woo

Bremen, Germany



Soobeen is a media artist based in Bremen, Germany, currently studying Digital Media at HfK Bremen. They primarily focus on observing the contradictory thinking that occurs when people narrowly perceive objects due to their anthropocentric behavior, aiming to incorporate this observation into various themes. They are particularly interested in shedding light on the invisible by highlighting it within the confines of human contradictory perspectives, thereby imbuing it with new attributes. Her work mainly revolves around media projects based on the web and virtual environments, as well as installation works that bridge the gap between virtual and real-world experiences.
