Natural collapse and natural healing

Individual Project
Projection Mapping
Ein-Kanal Video, Farbe, Ton
2023 / Germany



This work encapsulates the idea of nature collapsing due to external factors, yet naturally healing and recovering despite that. The work was created for the annual 'Lichter der City' in the city of Bremen and was exhibited from December 29, 2023 to January 14, 2024 at the HfK music building in Domshiede.
